RESPA Compliance

Listy operates fully within the guidelines of the Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA). Our flat fee model ensures transparency, no kickbacks, and clear disclosures for all parties involved.

We maintain strict compliance to protect consumers from unfair practices and to promote ethical real estate transactions.

FAQ - RESPA

What is RESPA?
The Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA) protects consumers by requiring disclosures about real estate settlement costs.
How does Listy comply with RESPA?
We only charge flat fees openly disclosed upfront. No referral fees or kickbacks occur.
Who enforces RESPA?
The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) enforces RESPA regulations.
